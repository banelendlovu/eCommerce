About Me

Hi, I'm Banele Ndlovu, CTO and Co-Founder of Koru Network. At Koru, I build data-driven tools and products designed to empower health-conscious users in maintaining healthier lives.

My passion lies in bridging the gap between business and technology through the power of data. From an early age, I've been fascinated by the business world, but it was my curiosity in data and tech that shaped my journey into solving real-world problems with data-centric solutions. Whether it's in the communities I've lived in or the companies I've worked with, I strive to create meaningful impact through innovation.

Beyond work, I'm a big fan of music and soccer. I played competitively from the age of 9 and continued through college at Waldorf University. Today, I channel that same drive and discipline into building analytical products that create value for people and their businesses.
